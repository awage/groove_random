Patterns

Here you can find some random grooves and also comping Exercises!!
The random grooves are based on syncopated 8th notes rhythm and paradidles. You can pick the bars that sounds better to you to build your own scores. Not all the generated rhythm have musical meaning. When playing paradiddles, do not forget to ghost the snare drum and mark the accents.

Comping exercises on the other hand should be played with a swing feeling. Play the swing pattern on the ride cymbal while practicing these rhythms. It should help you to improve your swing. See the book "The Art of Bop Drumming" to get more details on these rhythms. Have fun!
