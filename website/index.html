This web page is an educational ressource for drummers and percussionist in search for challenging exercises to improve their rhythm and their skills.

There are plenty of differents exercises, you can also play the tune, download the corresponding midi file and print or save the sheet.

You will find the following kind of exercises:
  • Reading at first sight with a large variety of figures and meters
  • Random comping and groove exercises to practice on a drum kit.
  • Sticking exercises to improve your technique.
  • Rhythm scales to practice polyrhythm habilities.


You will find a bar menu at the bottom of the page. In this menu you can generate the exercises and also change the display properties to adapt the sheet to your screen. You will also find three icons which are pretty self-explaining to listen to the tune, print the score and save the audio files.

The audio support is experimental. It will work only for cutting edge html5 browsers. Most of the other features work with a javascript capable internet browser.
