Rhythm scales

The rhythm scales are intended to devellop the regularity and the polyrhythm. They should be performed from beginning to te end back and forth. Start at very slow speed to ensure the accuracy, for example at 40 or 50 bpm. Choose a simple hands pattern and progressively switch to more complex ones. They can be played with different foot ostinatos. This material is explained in great details in the unreel drum book.
