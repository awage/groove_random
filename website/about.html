The drum legends

The inspiration to create this page came from great drum books. I recommend the reading and practicing of the following books:

  • Gary Chaffee's book series: Patterns.

  • Joe Morello: Master Study.

  • Marc Atkinson: The Unreel Drum Book.

  • Ted Reed: Syncopation.

  • John Riley: The Art of Bop Drumming.

  • George Lawrence: Stick Control for the Snare Drummer

  • Johnny Rabb: Jungle Drum n' Bass For the Acoustic Drum Set
